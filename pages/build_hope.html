What is Build Hope

Build Hope International is an organization founded by Pastor James Lee. Its mission is to BUILD orphanages and safe houses, RESCUE children caught in modern-day slavery, and RESTORE victims of slavery in countries such as Nicaragua, Africa, India, China, North Korea, and Cambodia.
Spero is thrilled to partner with an organization that shares the same vision for social justice and a team is planning on going out into the field alongside Build Hope International in 2014.
20% of the proceeds from Spero will be going toward a special Build Hope Dream fund in Cambodia, which pays for the college tuition for orphans. The goal is to raise $300,000 for medical school, nursing school, the police academy, and seminary school.
