Spero.

I hope.

We wanted to remind our audience of what Spero stands for - hope. Sometimes life isn't easy. We fall. We get discouraged. Fall again. And we feel like giving up.

But what gets us back up? Hope. It keeps us moving forward. It gives us courage to look up even when things get hard.

With that, our piece is simple. It is designed with the four letter word - hope, with an arrow through the O which points upwards towards the things that are to come and towards something that is better than what is present.

So don't be afraid (Matthew 10:31).

And have hope.
