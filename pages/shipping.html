Shipping & Handling

Spero ships from Berkeley, California. Spero ships free to the 48 continental U.S. states. Express shipping is not available. Orders will be shipped out within 2 business days of receiving the order. Once shipped most domestic orders take anywhere from 2 to 10 business days to arrive depending on the efficiency of your local postal service.

Return Policy

Unfortunately we do not do returns or exchanges because most of our clothes are vintage, unless the item is damaged.
