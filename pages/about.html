  • VISION
  • •
  • •

In Latin, the word “spero” means “to hope”. Spero is the collision of fashion and mission. Our vision is also inspired by Matthew 10:29-31, where something as insignificant as a sparrow, will be remembered and cared for. We want to expose the poverty in these neglected nations through an innovative medium. The goal of Spero is to bring hope to the hopeless and light into darkness. Spero is the 21st century means of bridging the fashion industry and missions field.

  • WHAT WE DO

    We are a team of UC Berkeley students dedicated to bridging fashion & mission. We source our clothes through various Bay Area thrift stores. Spero also has in-house designers that create original mission-driven clothing underlying our company’s purpose. Proceeds go toward various local and global charitable organizations.

  • LOCAL REACH

    ShopSpero partners with local organizations and businesses with like-minded philanthropic spirit. Speros team has been expanding its local reach by volunteering with Bay Area schools and non-profits. We're new to the local scene and continuously looking for local partners to support. Feel free to reach out at shopspero@gmail.com!

  • GLOBAL REACH

    Since its inception, Spero has supported developing countries through Southeast Asia. In 2013, our team partnered with Buildhope to provide resources and prayer to orphanages in Cambodia. Years later, we have expanded our vision to help underprivileged populations throughout the world, bridging fashion and mission.
